Beach Lover

This 8-day program is tailored for water and beach enthusiasts who prefer the rhythm of the waves over hiking trails. Discover Socotra’s breathtaking coastline, explore ancient fishing villages, and relax under the open sky in remote campsites.

From snorkeling with vibrant marine life to walking barefoot on untouched beaches, this adventure blends relaxation with exploration in a truly unique setting.

Note: This tour is designed for those who love the sea. Minimal hiking involved, maximum immersion in turquoise waters.
