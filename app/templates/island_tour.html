Full Island Tour 🌟 Socotra Essentials: 8-Day Island Discovery

Tour Duration: 8 days / 7 nights — Guided overland adventure with wild camping

🏕️ The Journey That Captures It All

This is our signature island experience — a curated journey through Socotra’s most iconic landscapes, from high plateaus and ancient forests to dazzling beaches and hidden freshwater pools.

You’ll sleep under the stars among Dragon’s Blood trees, swim in turquoise lagoons, snorkel vibrant reefs, and travel by boat to remote coastlines where dolphins may greet you along the way.

Although the itinerary is thoughtfully designed, we keep it flexible to ensure the best experience — adjusting routes when necessary due to weather or to avoid larger groups. Your comfort, safety, and immersion always come first.

🌍 Tour Highlights

  • Fermhin Forest: World’s highest density of Dragon’s Blood trees.
  • Detwah Lagoon: "The Magic Eye," with serene turquoise waters and white sands.
  • Shoab Beach: Boat-access only, ideal for dolphin and ray sightings.
  • Diksam Plateau: Hike through the island’s grandest canyon with panoramic views.
  • Homhil Infinity Pool: Cliffside spring pool with views of the sea and canyon.
  • Wadi Kalisan: Socotra’s largest natural swimming pool in a white limestone gorge.
  • Arher Beach: Where dunes, rivers, and cliffs meet — home to unique wildlife.
  • Ras Erissel: Eastern tip of the island where two oceans converge.
  • Hoq & Dugub Caves: Underground wonders filled with ancient formations.
  • Zahag Dunes: Towering dunes perfect for sunset views.
  • Amak Beach: Remote, private campsite on the Indian Ocean.
  • Dihamri Marine Reserve: Snorkel with turtles, coral fish, and moray eels.

✅ What’s Included

  • 8 days of fully supported camping
  • 4x4 Toyota Land Cruiser (max 3 guests per vehicle)
  • English-speaking local guide
  • All meals: freshly prepared daily + snacks and unlimited water
  • Camping gear: tent, mattress, blanket, pillow
  • All permits and entrance fees
  • Boat trip to Shoab Beach (weather permitting)
