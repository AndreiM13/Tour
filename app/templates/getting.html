Pack light but smart

Be sure to pack sunscreen, as it's not available on the island. Bring comfortable, breathable clothing, a sun hat for protection, and a headlamp for evenings. Most importantly, come with a good mood and a sense of adventure.

Local SIM

No roaming will work here. Etisalat sim card works in some places in Socotra (purchased from Abu Dhabi).

Cash

Cash is king on the island. Please ensure you only bring new USD notes with you.
