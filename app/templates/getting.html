How to get here?

Get in touch to arrange the tour by selection one of our packages

You will begin the process of getting here by sending us a small down payment and a picture of your passport. These necessities will be used to secure your tourist visa and flight from Abu Dhabi to Socotra.

A few weeks before your departure time, we will start processing the visa. *Your visa will arrive via email 2 weeks before departure to Abu Dhabi. You will need to have this printed and bring it along with your passport on the island

Pack your bags and fly to Abu Dhabi (the flight from your home to Abu Dhabi is not included). We advise you to arrive a day before flying to Socotra and leave enough time between your flight to Abu Dhabi and the one to Socotra in case of any delays,etc.
