Island Trekking: 8-Day Guided Trek with Camel Caravan

Immersive island crossing – wild camping, high peaks & Socotri hospitality

🥾 Cross the Island the Old Way

This is not your average island getaway. Our 8-day trek cuts across Socotra from north to south, led by seasoned local guides and accompanied by a camel caravan that carries your gear while you walk free beneath granite peaks and starlit skies.

The journey includes a 4-day crossing through the island’s highest mountain range (3-day option available), and the rest of the itinerary adapts to weather, movement of larger groups, and your pace — because the best adventures aren’t always scripted.

Note: A solid level of fitness is essential. You won’t need to carry your load, but you will be walking far off the beaten path.

🌍 Highlights Along the Way

  • Hajhir, Skand & Farah Mountains: Rugged cliffs, freshwater springs, ancient rocks.
  • Fermhin Forest: Dense grove of Dragon’s Blood Trees — otherworldly scenery.
  • Diksam Plateau: Dramatic canyons with sweeping island views.
  • Amak Beach: Finish your trek at a remote Indian Ocean beach.
  • Zahag Dunes & Dugub Cave: Golden sands and a cinematic cave adventure.
  • Detwah Lagoon: The “Magic Eye” — a turquoise basin with white sands.
  • Shoab Beach: Boat-access only, with dolphin encounters.
  • Homhil Infinity Pool: A natural pool spilling off the mountaintop.
  • Wadi Kalisan: Swim in a massive canyon-fed waterhole.
  • Arher Beach: Where cliffs, dunes, rivers and sea meet.
  • Ras Erissel: Eastern tip where two oceans collide.
  • Dihamri Marine Reserve: Snorkeling with turtles, eels, and coral fish.
