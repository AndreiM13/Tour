🌟 Socotra in Comfort: 8-Day Hotel-Based Island Tour

🏝️ What You’ll Experience

For travelers who want to explore the island’s wild beauty by day — and relax in hotel comfort by night. This all-inclusive journey blends daily adventures with air-conditioned rooms and delicious restaurant meals, ideal for those who want it all: nature, discovery, and ease.

We follow a carefully designed itinerary based on years of local insight, but we stay flexible — adjusting for weather and avoiding crowds to give you the best experience possible.

🌍 Tour Highlights

  • Fermhin Forest: Dragon’s Blood tree grove
  • Detwah Lagoon: “Magic Eye” turquoise bay
  • Shoab Beach: Remote beach, dolphin sightings*
  • Diksam Plateau: Highland canyon views
  • Homhil Infinity Pool: Cliffside natural spring
  • Wadi Kalisan: Socotra’s largest freshwater pool
  • Arher Beach: Cliffs, dunes, and rivers meet
  • Ras Erissel: Ocean and sea convergence
  • Zahag Dunes: Towering golden dunes
  • Hoq & Dugub Caves: Limestone caverns
  • Amak Beach: Hidden Indian Ocean gem + campsite option
  • Dihamri Marine Reserve: Snorkel with coral reef life
